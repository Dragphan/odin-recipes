Sushi

Description

You can always add your own twist to this recipe in order to spice it up, and add your own twist! This is a pretty simple recipe with simple ingredients. While some parts, like rolling the sushi may be difficult it'll be worth it in the end! Recipe sourced from allrecipes.com.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups water
  • 2/3 cup short-grain white rice
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 4 sheets nori seaweed sheets
  • 1/2 pound imitation crabmeat, flaked
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and sliced
  • 1/2 cucumber, peeled cut into small strips
  • 2 tablespoons pickled ginger

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 300°F (150°C).
  2. Boil the water in a medium pot, and stir in the rice. Then reduce the heat to medium-low, cover it, and simmer until the rice is tender and water has been absorbed, 20 t0 25 minutes. Or use a rice cooker to prepare it.
  3. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a bowl, gently stir into the cooked rice in the pot and set it aside.
  4. Lay the nori sheets on a baking sheet.
  5. Heat nori in the preheated oven until warm, 1-2 minutes.
  6. Center 1 nori sheet on a bamboo sushi mat (lay a plastic film between the bamboo mat and the nori so that the rice doesn't get stuck in the bamboo mat). Use wet hands to spread a thin layer of rice on top. Arrange 1/4 of the crabmeat, avocado, cucumber, and pickled ginger over rice in a line down the center.
  7. Lift one end of the mat and roll it tightly over filling to make a complete roll. Repeat with the remaining ingredients.
  8. Use a wet, sharp knife to cut each roll into 4-6 slices, depending on how wide you like your sushi.
